CORE VALUES

Continuous Improvement

By holding weekly team development meetings in the new work area we make sure that everyone's voice is heard. The meetings will be conducted until the best practices have been decided in the new work area. This practice encourages open communication, effectiveness and the ability to influence the workflow.

Systematically

We strive for systematical work by using checklists. The purpose is to maintain consistency, accuracy, and make sure that no cleaning area is forgotten. As a customer, you will receive a copy of the checklist meant for constructive feedback. With the feedback, we can then correct mistakes immediately and enjoy our high quality accomplishment.

Unity

Every team members voice needs to be heard. When everyone's ideas and best practices are considered, it nurtures a growing environment where everyone can influence their own work. It is important for us to work as a team and discuss matters openly. All our employees speak English.

Communication

We always have a person ready to answer the phone. If a problem occurs, we will try to help as soon as possible.

OUR SERVICES

Home Cleaning

We clean all the rooms in the house, eg. bedrooms, living rooms, kitchen and bathrooms. We clean from the cleanest to the dirtiest, then the cleaning stays as hygienic as possible.

Window Cleaning

We offer the best quality and care for your home. Our principle is to wash your windows as if they were our own. We remove tape, paint, insects, minerals or oxidation stains during our normal washing.

Gardening

We clean your garden, remove debris and leaves by keeping your garden tidy. We believe that gardens work best when they are usable and easy to maintain.

Laundry

Before doing the laundry, we first sort the clothes, delicately wash them with our gentle cleaning agents depending on the care the clothe requires, and fold them accordingly.

Car Cleaning

We service any type of cars. You can choose whether from our services such as exterior wash, interior cleaning, carpet shampooing, and hand washing.
